Our Product

Our product, the cleaning crepe, was concieved by our managing director Liv during an very productive meeting. It is designed to be a more accessible way of removing mess via foot, rather than using other more vulnerable muscles like your back. We intend for our product to help those with movement disabilities by providing a safer, easier and more fun way of cleaning!

The Product will come in various sizes for all various types of feet; with many colour variations so that you may clean with panache and style!
